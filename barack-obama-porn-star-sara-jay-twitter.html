Barack Obama follows porn star Sara Jay on Twitter and people are losing it The US Sun

Publish date: 2024-06-24

TWITTER went into meltdown after savvy social media users appeared to have noticed that porn star Sara Jay had a very powerful follower.

Sara, 42, has 1.1 million followers on Twitter - with tone of them apparently former US president Barack Obama.

The porn star, who also runs a porn production company, began trending on Twitter - and when people wondered why they discovered her famous fan.

"Why does former President Obama follow porn star Sara Jay?" asked one Twitter user.

While another added: "Finding out that Barack Obama follows Sara Jay is exactly what the world needed today."

Obama, 58, follows more than 608,000 accounts and people from all walks of life, but Twitter users rushed to assume the president followed her because he enjoyed her onscreen talents.

Sara who was inducted into the Adult Video News (AVN) Hall of Fame, put the speculation to bed by giving her thoughts on why Obama followed her.

"Maybe it's because I produce, direct, write and manage adult talent for my production company Wyde Syde".

That didn't stop hundreds of hilarious memes featuring the president, joking about his now infamous follow.

The social media conversation then switched to how the former president wife, Michelle Obama would react to him following a porn star on Twitter.

"Michelle’s about to do MORE than snatching Barack Obama phone. He’s finna be IN TROUBLE. He better unfollow Sara Jay," one Twitter wrote with an accompanying GIF of Michelle snatching her husband's phone out of his hand.

Another Twitter user agreed, following Sara might get him into trouble: "Barack my good brother, we love you. But I gotta ask, did you unfollow Sara Jay yet? Because if the black queen Michelle Obama finds out? It’s going to be beyond us, and we won’t be able to save you."

But Obama following Sara is not new news, with the porn star bragging all the way back in 2015 that she had a famous follower.

“Everybody watches porno,” she told AllHipHop.com and adding, “Obama follows me on Twitter! I’m just saying.”

Along with being the CEO of her production company, Sara also writes, directs and produces adult films, along with managing other porn stars.

She has worked in the industry for two decades and was one of the most searched for porn stars in 2018, according to the Daily Beast.

Sara decided to capitalise on the renewed interest in her and scheduled a new cam show for her followers writing on Twitter "I'm apparently trending. You can see exactly why when I'm on Cam Soda Live".

Obama appears to have now unfollowed Sara after the Twitter frenzy yesterday.
