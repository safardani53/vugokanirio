Boy, 7, dies after falling off pub's garden wall in Harold Wood, East London

Publish date: 2024-07-28

A SEVEN-year-old boy has died after falling off a pub wall in East London.

The little boy is understood to have been in the beer garden when the tragedy unfolded at the King Harold Pub in Romford yesterday afternoon.

The London Ambulance Service found the lad to be suffering from severe injuries about 5.20pm.

But the schoolboy was tragically pronounced dead shortly after he arrived at the hospital around an hour later.

Cops are treating the death as "unexplained" and the Health and Safety Executive has been informed.

A Met Police spokesperson said: "The death is currently being treated as unexplained.
