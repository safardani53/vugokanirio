Lisa Vanderpump launches new villa-set spinoff show after VPRs ratings explode from scandal- with

Publish date: 2024-06-02

VANDERPUMP Rules has gotten a spin-off that will take place in Lisa Vanderpump's villa.

One day after the VPR reunion dropped on Bravo, Lisa announced her next adventure.

The spin-off will feature 10 episodes, streaming on Hulu, called Vanderpump Villa.

The show is "a new unscripted confection that takes place at the reality star’s luxury French villa," according to Deadline.

Vanderpump Villa does not have an air date as of yet.

The new reality show will "follow her hand-picked staff as they live and work together to navigate every extravagant desire of their well-to-do guests," the outlet reports.

Lisa will executive produce the reality show, as well as Entertainment 360.

Bunim/Murray Productions, who produces The Challenge, The Family Stallone, and more, will also serve as producers.

Aside from staring on and executive producing VPR and the spin-off, Villa, Lisa also EP's Overserved on E!, Vanderpump Dogs on Peacock, and Pooch Perfect on ABC.

She and her husband, Ken Todd, also have their hands in the restaurant and nightclub business.

BREAKING RECORDS

The aftermath of Tom Sandoval and Raquel Leviss' affair did wonders for VPR's ratings.

The finale numbers proved that scandal brings higher viewership.

Within three days, 4.1million viewers tuned in to the finale on Bravo, Peacock, and On-Demand.

2.4million of those fans were in the 18 to 49 age range, according to Yahoo! Entertainment.

This made season 10 become the most watched of VPR ever.
