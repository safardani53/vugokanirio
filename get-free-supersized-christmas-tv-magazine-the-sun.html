Get your FREE supersized Christmas TV Magazine in The Sun this Saturday

Publish date: 2024-08-13

CHRISTMAS is coming early for Sun readers as TV Mag is gifting you two magazines packed full of festive content.

TV Mag's 120-page double issue features exclusive interviews as well as 14 days of complete listings from Dec 24 through to Jan 6.

It includes over 200 new shows and interviews with their stars, the A-Z of festive film favourites as well as sport and movie planners.

There is also a kids show guide and a look at 23 of the best shows coming in 2023.

All your festive favourites feature in the bumper issue, including the Christmas Specials for Call The Midwife, Strictly Come Dancing and The Great British Bake Off.

Martin Clunes chats about the last ever episode of Doc Martin, while Brendan O'Carroll teases what fun and games can be expected from this year's Mrs Brown's Boys Christmas Special.

Ronan Keating also chats about joining The Voice Kids, while Alex Jones opens up about her new programme, Alex Jones: Making Babies.

If that wasn't enough, there's also a double issue of TV Soap to look forward to on December 18.

The 48-page special includes 14 days of listings from 18 - 31 December as well as all the soap storylines and interviews with the stars of the soaps.

These include EastEnders' Kellie Bright, Charlie Brooks and Shane Richie.
