Josh Duggars childhood friend Clint Branham to tell all on forensics from child pornography trial

Publish date: 2024-02-28

JOSH Duggar’s childhood friend is giving his expert opinion on the computer forensics discussed at the disgraced star’s child pornography trial on The SoJo Files podcast. 

Clint Branham has been at his current Technical and Cybersecurity Expert position for nearly seven years, as his experience in technology dates back to 2004. 

Clint shut down Josh’s legal team’s theory that he isn’t technically savvy enough to access the dark web to download and view child pornography when he took the stand during the nearly two-week trial. 

While on the stand for prosecution, Clint called Josh “more advanced than an average user” and said he was “comfortable” installing his own programs, as he was known as the “tech guy” in his family. 

During the trial, prosecution was able to prove Josh was at the car lot when child pornography was downloaded through texts, photos and GPS tracking.

But Michele Bush, the defense’s forensics expert, presented the possibility of remote access in an attempt to exonerate Josh. 

Now, Clint will walk listeners through the key technical aspects discussed during the trial on The SoJo Files.

He will help listeners better grasp the technical terms and descriptions discussed during the trial. 

The episode is expected to drop following the holidays, with additional episodes that will wrap up the trial. 

Another upcoming episode on the podcast will feature a Court Appointment Special Advocate worker and volunteer from the Northwest Arkansas region.

He is currently in jail awaiting sentencing and faces 20 years in prison for each of the two counts. 

True crime fans may also enjoy the podcast’s upcoming episodes on the Jordan Shreeve case, where a man was put on trial and found not guilty of the first-degree murder of a two-year-old girl.

TikTok legal expert Carrie Jernigan will also appear on upcoming episodes to help cover two unsolved Arkansas cases. 

The SoJo Files is run by an anonymous husband and wife duo.
