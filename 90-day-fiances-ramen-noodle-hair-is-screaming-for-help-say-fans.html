90 Day Fiances ramen noodle hair is screaming for help, say fans Mon 18 December 2023

Publish date: 2024-07-08

Sophie Sierra’s romance with Rob Warne isn’t the only thing receiving criticism – her hair is currently under a ruthless attack from 90 Day Fiance fans.

From keeping their sexualities a secret to infidelity accusations, 90 Day Fiance stars Sophie Sierra and Rob Warne have encountered countless relationship roadblocks on the TLC show. As the cast members put their love issues on TV, it has exposed them to the harsh reality of the internet and Sophie’s blonde hair is being ripped to shreds on social media – but not literally.

Sophie’s hair is ‘irking the spirit’ of 90 Day Fiance fans

23-year-old British native Sophie Sierra is recognizable thanks to her bleach blonde hair and long wigs, but they haven’t received raving reviews online. The latest 90 Day episode (December 17) saw the Instagram influencer wear a gold and black patterned jacket with her bleach blonde hair styled in two space buns as she browsed an adult shop with Rob.

Brutal fans were quick to point out Sophie’s dark roots peeking out from her parting – and they’re pointing fingers at Rob:

Fans may be extra critical as Sophie has admitted to living a lavish life, thus explaining why she was taken aback by Rob’s small apartment which only had a bathroom outside. With her economic status, fans believe she can splash a little more cash on her hairstyles.

Sophie hails from a wealthy family thanks to her grandfather, but is unclear on his occupation. “I grew up wealthy and lived in big houses,” she told Rob. The reality star attended a boarding school and lived in Spain and the UK.

TV stars claps back to ‘cheap wig’ hate

One thing’s for sure, Sophie isn’t the type to let the haters go wild. Following countless commetns attacking her blonde wig, the TLC cast member took to Instagram to prove her pricey ponytail.

“For the dumba** comments saying I wore a cheap wig on the show. It was $1000 from a quality hair store in London,” she retaliated. “I didn’t know anything about wigs so maybe I laid it wrong or something idk, but it defo wasn’t a cheap or synthetic one.”

The comment was posted alongside a screenshot of $1001 long blonde wavy wig from A-List Lace Hair.

Sophie’s natural brunette locks are a hit

TV fans have only seen Sophie with platinum locks but with season 10 nearing its end, the influencer has ditched the Barbie look for her natural brown hair after two years of bleach “ruining her life”.

“She looks great with her natural color. Beautiful. Honestly, I’m over the fake stuff, eyelashes , hair extensions, etc! So much prettier natural,” a Reddit fan praised.
