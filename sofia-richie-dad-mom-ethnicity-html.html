Meet Sofia Richie Famous Mom &amp; Dad, Also Find Out Her Ethnicity &amp; Facts

Publish date: 2024-06-21

Sofia Richie is an upcoming model with big contracts in her kitty. She has worked with Elle Magazine, Teen Vogue, Elle Girl - Japan, Nylon, Fault, Dazed, Love Culture magazine and Olay. She has also worked as the new face of the pop legend Madonna’s clothing line, Material Girl. She also works with Alex Avant, entertainment executive, son of music and entertainment legend, Clarence Avant. At only 17, Sofia is already climbing to the top of the modeling industry. She recently shot editorial spreads for V. Galore, Clam, and C- magazines and is courted for a TV show.

Sofia Richie’s Age, Ethnicity & Net Worth

American model Sofia was born on August 24, 1998, in Los Angeles, California, the USA as the daughter of a musician, Lionel Richie, and Diane Alexander. She also has an older brother named Miles. She also has an elder half-sister named Nicole Richie who is a fashionista and an entrepreneur (daughter of Lionel and his then-wife Brenda Harvey-Richie). She considers Nicole as her second mom.

Being the daughter of a famous dad and having a successful sister, Sofia, with a height of 5 feet and 5 inches, decided to carve out a stable niche for herself among the hottest glitterati of Hollywood and Fashion world and pursued a modeling career. 

When it comes to her modeling career, Sofia started her career by making her debut on Nicole's hit TV show, Candidly Nicole (2014) on VH-1 at age 15. Right after the show, she got a chance to work in Who What Wear, a feature in Elle Magazine, the cover story for Nation Alist Magazine, being the face of Mary Grace Swim, and teaming up with Teen Vogue and Olay. Since then, her career has sky-rocketed, and so has her net worth of $3 million.

Years after that relationship ended, she was linked up with some few names such as Jake Andrews, French soccer player Samir Nasri, Canadian singer Justin Bieber, Anwar Hadid, model Brooklyn Beckham. All of those relationships could only last for a few months.
