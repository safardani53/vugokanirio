Sarah Michelle Gellar Joins Cast Of Original Series Wolf Pack

Publish date: 2024-06-27

Announced at San Diego Comic-Con 2022, Gellar joins the pack alongside Armani Jackson, Bella Shepard, Chloe Rose Robertson, and Tyler Lawrence Gray.

Sarah Michelle Gellar is diving back into the world of all things supernatural. This time, she'll be hanging out with werewolves as she has officially joined the cast of the upcoming Paramount+ Original series, Wolf Pack. 

STREAM: Catch Up On Every Episode Of Teen Wolf On Paramount+

Announced during a surprise appearance at the Teen Wolf: The Movie panel in Hall H at San Diego Comic-Con, Gellar will join previously announced Wolf Pack stars Armani Jackson (Honor Society), Bella Shepard (iCarly, The Wilds), Chloe Rose Robertson (Wildflower), and Tyler Lawrence Gray (Our Town, Macbeth).

Based on the book series by Edo Van Belkom, Wolf Pack follows a teenage boy and girl whose lives are forever changed when a California wildfire awakens a terrifying supernatural creature. Gellar stars as arson investigator Kristin Ramsey, a highly regarded expert in her field and no stranger to personal loss, brought in by authorities to catch the teenage arsonist who started a massive wildfire which may have also led to the reawakening of a supernatural predator terrorizing Los Angeles.

Original Teen Wolf creator Jeff Davis is set to write and executive produce the series. Davis also wrote and produced the forthcoming Paramount+ original film, Teen Wolf: The Movie. 

Wolf Pack is slated to premiere later this year in the U.S., Canada, Latin America, the Caribbean, Australia, South Korea, Ireland and the UK. It will also be available in Italy, Germany, Austria, Switzerland, and France.
