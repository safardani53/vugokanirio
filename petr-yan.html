Petr Yan Age, Biography

Petr Yan was born on February 11, 1993, with the full name Petr Evgenyevich Yan. He is from Dudinka in Russia’s Krasnoyarsk Krai. He is Russian by nationality and Mixed by ethnicity, having been born to a father of mixed Chinese-Georgian ancestry and a Russian mother. As of 2023, he is 30 years old. He has not revealed any information about his parents. Only the fact that he has an older brother is known.

In sixth grade, Petr Yan began training in ITF Taekwon-do and frequently fought in the streets and at his local school, so his family frequently relocated to try to prevent this. He continued to train in boxing for 8 years, eventually earning the title of Master of Sport in Boxing in the 64 kg weight category. He studied Physical Culture and Sports at Siberian Federal University and graduated with honors.
