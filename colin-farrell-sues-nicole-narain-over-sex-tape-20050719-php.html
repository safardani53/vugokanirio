Colin Farrell sues Nicole Narain over sex tape

Publish date: 2024-09-17
Colin Farrell sues Nicole Narain over sex tape / Jossip



Tue / 19 Jul 2005 Colin Farrell sues Nicole Narain over sex tape » More Colin Farrell

It looks like Colin Farrell will not be taking part in the sale of his 14-minute sex tape with former Playboy Bunny Nicole Narain.

Colin filed suit against his former fling, arguing she’s trying to release the tape through a middleman even though the duo supposedly agreed to share ownership of the footage and keep it private.

The pair linked to the tape’s sale that Narain is allegedly partnering with are a one “J.J.” (who contacted the New York Post for advice) and newcomer Paul Nash, who denies he’s trying to sell the video.
