Chantel Jeffries Biography

Internationally renowned model Chantel Jeffries is mainly recognized for her association with the pop star Justin Bieber. She is currently, signed to Wilhelmina Modeling agency.

Jeffries was born on 1st October 1992 in California and grew up in Jacksonville, North Caroline along with her sister Selah. Talking about her nationality, she is an American, and Jeffries belongs to the African-American ethnicity.

To date, Chantell has appeared in three movies such as “A Weekend with the family”, “E! News”, and “The Perfect Match”.
