Who is Joanna Krupa's husband, Douglas Nunes?

Publish date: 2024-06-05

POLISH model, Joanna Krupa, has been married to businessman, Douglas Nunes, since 2018.

On March 27, 2023, it was revealed that Nunes had filed for divorce from his wife.

Who is Joanna Krupa's husband Douglas Nunes?

Douglas Nunes is the founding Partner & CEO of the company, 451 Media Group, according to his LinkedIn page.

He has been with the entertainment network for nearly 9 years, which includes co-founders: Michael Bay and John & Anthony Gentile.

According to his bio, Nunes has "20+ years of experience in executive management in the entertainment, digital media, and financial services industries."

Krupa and Nunes got married in 2018 in Krakow, Poland.

Are they getting divorced?

On March 27, 2023, TMZ reported that Nunes had filed for divorce from Krupa - citing irreconcilable differences - according to Los Angeles County court documents.

The pair have allegedly been separated since January 2, 2023.

According to the outlet: "When it comes to spousal support, the guy's asking a judge to leave Joanna out in the cold ... as he's looking to block the court's ability to award her any of that.

"DN notes in his paperwork that he wants the court to determine who gets what from their marital assets, and there's no mention of a prenup in the petition."

How many children do Krupa and Nunes have?

The pair welcomed their first child, daughter, Asha-Leigh Nunes, back in 2019.

Krupa took to Instagram in May of 2019 to announce the news with a picture of herself smiling as she held a positive pregnancy test.

According to court documents obtained by TMZ, Nunes is allegedly asking for joint legal and physical custody of their daughter.

If the couple goes through with the divorce, it will mark the Real Housewives of Miami star second failed marriage.

Krupa was previously married to businessman Romain Zago from 2013 to 2017.
