Publish date: 2024-03-17

RYAN Reynolds has announced the reboot of ALF, an '80s sitcom he'll be using to create custom ads on his Maximum Effort channel.

The actor took to Twitter to share a brief clip of a custom ALF ad along with the official reveal of what he had planned.

Ryan tweeted: "Holy cats! Having our own
@MaximumEffort Channel means doing fun things like making new ALF content for ALF re-runs. Marketing should be FUN."

The Maximum Effort channel on FUBO, a streaming service, will be airing re-runs of the classic '80s sitcom.

ALF is a sitcom about the titular "Alien Life Form" who comes to live with an unsuspecting family.

The classic show is played by a furry puppet and the show quickly became a hit while airing.
