AMANDA Cerny has made a name for herself from YouTube.

Here’s everything we know about the internet personality.

Who is Amanda Cerny? 

Amanda Rachelle Cerny is an American YouTuber, actress and model. 

She is best known for her YouTube channels, Amanda Cerny and Amanda Cerny Vlogs. 

She has 24.8 million on her Instagram account, which she describes as ‘Your daily dose of happiness’, and a further 1.1 million followers on Twitter.

Her YouTube channel has 2.77 million subscribers.

Originally from Pittsburgh, the stunning 30 year old grew up in Florida and attended Florida State University. 

Amanda began working as a model when she was 15, and was soon featured in Playboy. 

She had a huge following of 4.6 million on Vine. 

What is Amanda Cerny’s net worth? 

Cerny is estimated to be worth $8 million.

She has built up a seriously impressive multi million dollar career through social media and endorsing brands such as Tinder and Paramount Pictures. 

Cerny loves to turn her hand to a good cause and is passionate about charity work.

She founded the Play Foundation, a non profit organisation which helps children to connect with music. 

She also works in areas affected by natural disasters and is a proud ambassador of the UN. 

When did Amanda Cerny and Logan Paul date? 

The pair started dating in 2016. 

They lived very public lives, and to the delight of their fans, their romance was no exception and they frequently documented their relationship online. 

They regularly posted sweet vlogs together when they were dating, and their videos together gained millions of views. 

They eventually split in 2017 after a year together. 

Despite their break up, Cerny has remained sweet about Paul.
