Rick Ross takes savage swipe at DJ Envy's wife in shocking new video

RICK Ross has thrown major shade at DJ Envy in a wild new video about the rapper's wife shared on Twitter.

CAR SHOW RIVALRY

Rick Ross and DJ Envy have entered an all-out battle this year regarding their competing car shows.
