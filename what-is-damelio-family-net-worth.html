D'Amelio family: What is their net worth?

Publish date: 2024-02-28

CHARLI and Dixie D'Amelio rose to fame thanks to their TikTok success and have since turned their family into what some may say are the next Kardashian bunch.

Due to their success, the D'Amelio family has been able to amass an extensive net worth and many want to know how high their price tag actually is.

What is the D'Amelio family's net worth?

Over the years, the D'Amelio family has only become more popular among fans and they are now a household name with their own Hulu show.

While an exact net worth for the family remains unclear, individually, they are millionaires.

As of February 2022, Charli, 17, has an estimated net worth of $20million, according to Celebrity Net Worth, while Dixie, 20, has a respectable $10million net worth.

This number comes from their various business ventures as well as social media success.

Marc D'Amelio, 53, is their father and also has an extensive net worth thanks to the family fame. He is estimated to have a net worth anywhere from $1.5million to $2million.

At this time, it remains unclear what Heidi D'Amelio's, 50, net worth is.

Will there be a second season of The D'Amelio Show?

In September 2021, Hulu released a new reality TV series called The D'Amelio Show, which follows the lives of the D'Amelio family as they try to navigate their new stardom.

Season one had eight episodes and concluded with Charli's 17th birthday but at this time, it remains unclear if there will be a second season.

Along with The D'Amelio show, Hulu also released The Kardashians after Keeping Up With the Kardashians ended on E!.
