Publish date: 2024-06-18

ROB Kardashian has given fans a look at his St. Patrick's Day surprise birthday cake, complete with a rare photo of the star.

The Kardashian star keeps a much lower profile than the rest of his family, avoiding the public eye.

Rob, 36, made a rare appearance on social media, giving his fans a glimpse into his birthday celebration.

The reality star shared a photo of his birthday cake which was St. Patrick's Day-themed.

Fashioned to look like a box of Lucky Charms, the celebratory dessert featured a photo of Rob in place of the leprechaun that usually appears on the box.

Next to the cake was lettering that read: "Happy birthday, Rob."

The star added no further context to the post.

He did, however, share a few more peeks into the party.

Rob posted a photo alongside Khloe Kardashian, who shared the same photo of the cake on her own Instagram Story.

He included a camera emoji and a blue heart.

His caption appeared to suggest that the bash was perhaps being filmed for The Kardashians season three, though it's not entirely clear.

Kris Jenner also appeared to be present, appearing in the reflection of the photo.

MOTHER'S LOVE

The Kardashian momager shared a sweet tribute to her son on social media.

Kris posted a series of throwback photos of Rob, starting with one of him wearing a Barney costume.

She wrote: "Happy birthday to my amazing wonderful beautiful son, Rob!"

The star then posted a photo of herself, her late husband Robert Kardashian Sr, and Rob as a baby.

She captioned the snap: "You are one of the most amazing humans I know in life."

She then shared a throwback pic of herself and a young Rob, writing: "You are so kind, loving, smart, creative, generous, giving, and one of the nicest and most humble men I have ever known."

Posting a photo of Rob and his daughter Dream together when she was a baby, Kris gushed: "You are the greatest dad in the world! You are so amazing and fabulous with Dream!"

She then shared a snap seemingly from Dream's birthday, continuing: "You make sure her every need and desire is taken care of constantly and you do it with such joy, passion, consistency, and love."

Kris posted yet another photo of herself and her son from his early childhood, this time riding on a carousel.

She wrote: "Thank you Rob for all the passion, all the love, all the kindness that you give all of us every single day."

In another post, she wrote: "I thank God every single day that He chose me to be your mom."

She went on in another slide: "I am the luckiest mother in the world to have you as my son."

Kris concluded: "I feel completely blessed, and I love you more than you will ever know."

SIBLING SUPPORT

Khloe shared a tribute of her own that rivaled her mom's.

She, too, shared several throwback photos of the reclusive star on Instagram.

A black-and-white snapshot showed the Hulu star showing off her signature pout while Rob smiled beside her.

Another photo showed the pair as kids sitting together at a restaurant. 

The Keeping Up With the Kardashians alum also included a brief video of Rob as an infant, sporting an outfit for both his birthday and St. Patrick's Day, alongside their late father. 

Khloe captioned the post: "It’s one of my most favorite days because it’s one of my Most favorite human being’s birthday!! My baby bobby boy.

She continued: "My wish for you is a thousand wishes And for them all to come true. That you wake up one day and you know with everything inside your being that you are enough. You’ve always been enough.

"My wish is that you feel what anyone feels when they are in your presence, complete bliss and respect! You make people feel safe and that they are home when they are around you. I pray you realize that WE are the lucky ones because we have you."

She went on to write that her birthday wish for her brother will be "deeply and sublimely happy," and for "deep peaceful happiness" to enter Rob's life and never leave his side.

Khloe wrote: "My wish is that you continue to do things that push you outside your comfort zone. Let this season be what it needs to be. Let it shape you how it is destined to. Tell people that you love them, often, and that you appreciate them. Be the greatest person you can possibly be. Do things that make you proud."

She advised her brother to "believe" in himself and his "journey" and to "continue to be humble."

"Let go of the pressure that your past has put on you. Thrive at your own pace. The secret is, just be better than you were yesterday. The desire is to Do it all for you and you alone."

The Kardashians star confessed that she "couldn’t be prouder" to call Rob her brother, before adding, "I couldn’t be luckier that I get to call you whenever I want about absolutely nothing."
