Eloise Webb Biography

Publish date: 0001-01-01

Webb is now aged thirteen years old. She is now four feet and nine inches in her height and her nationality is English. She is spending her days with her parents.

Webb has been active as an actress since 2011. She made her acting debut that year by starring the child role in the drama and history movie The Iron Lady. The following year she appeared in the movie Red Lights. She has also starred in the movie Bel Ami. She got her rise in fame after she portrayed the young version of the title character in the 2015 movie version of Cinderella. To date, she has starred in several movies. Her net worth is $1.5 million as of 2022.
