Former Open champion John Daly hangs out in Hooters with host of busty waitresses after missing the

Publish date: 2024-03-28

FORMER Open champion John Daly has been pictured hanging out at Hooters after missing the cut at Hoylake.

The 57-year-old - who is a huge fan of the American fast food chain - hung out with busty waitresses during his trip after heading to a nearby branch.

Daly hit 12 bogeys, one double bogey and two birdies across his two rounds at the Open.

His score of 12 over par was not enough to make the cut, so he instead headed to Hooters, who also sponsor him.

He posed for pictures with fans and waiting staff during the visit, as well as signing autographs.

Daly had been wearing Hooters branded pants on the course this week, but changed into jeans for his visit to the restaurant.

"Long John" is a self-confessed Hooters fan and regularly visits the chain.

He even popped over for chicken wings during last year's PGA Championship in Oklahoma.

When he is not at Hooters or on the course, though, Daly also writes and records his own music.

The former Open champion has released three albums of his own in addition to providing backing vocals for other artists.
