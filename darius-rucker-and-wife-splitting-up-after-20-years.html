Darius Rucker and wife splitting up after 20 years but say family is top priority The US Sun

Publish date: 2024-09-09

DARIUS Rucker and his wife, Beth, announced they are going separate ways after 20 years of marriage.

The two said despite splitting up, their family would remain their priority.

Darius posted on his Instagram: "Beth and I would like to share that after much reflection we have made the decision to consciously uncouple. We remain close friends and parenting partners and continue to be each other’s biggest cheerleaders.

"Our priority will always be our beautiful family. We have so much love in our hearts for each other and will continue to encourage growth and expansion in one other.

"Please be kind as we take on this journey, and we thank you for your love and support always. — Darius & Beth Rucker"

Darius and Beth have two children, Daniella, 19, and son Jack, 15.

The Hootie & the Blowfish frontman also has a daughter, Caroline, 25, from a prior relationship.

Last year, Darius and Beth started the Just Be You organization to improve children's confidence.

According to Wide Open Country, the former couple met in 1998.

Beth was working for VH1 in New York, and Darius was performing in town.

The rocker told The Boot in 2010 his and Beth's had an instantaneous connection.

Darius said: "She was walking up the stairs [to her apartment], and I said, 'Well, you know that I'm going to marry you, right?' She just laughed."

He recalled: "Her roommate or her boyfriend was up there in their apartment, and I yelled, 'To whoever's up there in Beth Leonard's apartment, you can just leave because I just want you to know that I'm going to marry her!'"

Darius and Beth did indeed get married in 2000.

At the time Darius told The Boot: "It takes a strong woman to be married to a musician, especially a musician who was drinking and partying as hard as I was, and I think life for us is where we are now.

"It took a long time for us to get to where we are now, and we say to each other all the time, 'I love us. I love our family. I love us.'"
