Harry Styles and Rihanna: Dating?!

Publish date: 2024-04-23

She may tend to go for bad boys (her ex Chris Brown is in jail at the moment), but if anyone can get Rihanna to change her ways and settle down it’s Harry Styles. And according to friends, that’s just what the One Direction star intends to do.

Following his failed romance with Kendall Jenner (and Taylor Swift, and probably numerous other young starlets), Harry has set his sites on the troubled RiRi say sources close to his band.

"The girl he would like to get with is definitely Rihanna," says the source. "He always had a a thing for her, but they don’t really know each other. But who knows what could happen in the future."

 

Well, there’s good news and bad news for Harry.

The good news is, RiRi told a London tabloid last year that she has a crush on Mr. Styles. On the other hand, there’s a 6-foot Canadian road block to their romance in the form of Drake.

Rihanna has been spending a lot of time with the rapper recently and has even joined him on the recent European leg of his tour. We suggest you sit this one out for now, Harry. Drake may be soft by rapper standards, but you still don’t want that beef.
