Who is JellyRoll:

JellyRoll is a famous Rapper. He was born on December 4, 1986 and his birthplace is Antioch, Nashville, TN. JellyRoll is also well known as, Jason DeFord.

He released his first album, Halfway House in 2015. Later he released several other mixtapes and emerged as a rapper. He released a mixtape named Addiction Kills in 2016. He released another album A Beautiful Disaster in 2020. This album was a huge hit and debuted no# 97 on the US Billboard 200.

Some of his popular singles are I’m So High, This Down Here and Train Tracks.

JellyRoll is originated from United States. He frequently collaborates with fellow rappers Lil Wyte and Haystak.
