Pregnant Katy Perry gives fans 'sneak peek' of daughter's pink-themed nursery ahead of her due date

Publish date: 2024-03-08

PREGNANT Katy Perry gave her fans a glimpse of her daughter's pink-themed nursery.

The Fireworks singer, 35, is expecting her first child with fiancé Orlando Bloom any day.

Excited to meet her impending arrival, Katy decided to give her fans "just a little sneak peek" at her nursery during her weekly Smile Sunday live stream.

The California Girls hitmaker said: "Hey everyone, I'm going to show you my baby room" as she carried her laptop in her hands.

Panning over some "little clothes" on display, the songstress showcased the impending little one's outfits displayed on circular hooks, including a onesie with Orlando's face all over it.

Katy then continued to showcase the room's bubblegum pink-theme, which included a light pink recliner, crib and coordinating mobile.

Aside from the furniture, the room's window boasted lavender-toned drapes and pentagon-shaped light fixtures and white floating self-displayed children's books area.

Before rounding off the tour, Katy gave a glimpse of the changing area she had designed.

Katy and Orlando announced that they're expecting their first child, his second, together back in March 2020.

Although she hasn't given her exact due date, she said in an Instagram post following her announcement: "Let's just say it's gonna be a jam-packed summer."

The couple first met at Harvey Weinstein's Golden Globes after-party in 2016.

However, Orlando previously warned they're "not by any means the kind of Hollywood love story you might want to hear about."
