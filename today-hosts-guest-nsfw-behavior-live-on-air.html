TODAY hosts had to remind an A-list guest that she was on a morning show when she started twerking live on air.

The Split star was dressed in a chic pinstriped jumpsuit as she showed off her sexy twerking skills.

"What are you doing?! host Dylan Dreyer was heard shouting, as Sheinelle continued: "We have been waiting for this, star Haley Lu Richardson live in studio 1A, she's taking us behind the scenes of all your favourite..."
