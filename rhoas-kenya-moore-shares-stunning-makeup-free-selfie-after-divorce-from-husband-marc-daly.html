RHOAs Kenya Moore shares stunning makeup-free selfie after divorce from husband Marc Daly The

Publish date: 2024-05-19

REAL Housewives of Atlanta star Kenya Moore shared a beautiful selfie where she's rocking the no-makeup look.

The mother of one took to Instagram yesterday to show off her glowy skin and natural curls in a bikini selfie, amid her divorce from baby daddy Marc Daly.

Along with the picture, the housewife wrote the words "Open" with a heart emoji.

The caption could be a reference to how she feels after her split from husband Marc.

The two announced in September 2019 that they were getting a divorce back after two years of marriage.

This bare-faced selfie is a rarity on Kenya's Instagram account as fans are used to seeing her in a full face of makeup.

Most recently, she posted a picture of herself in a bikini and even then, she was rocking lashes and a full set of abs.

Typically, Kenya can be spotted with a smokey eye look, glossy lips, and her hair blown out.

After their impending divorce made news, Kenya and Marc attributed their marriage ending to a variety of issues, from lack of intimacy to the fact that they are mainly in a long distance relationship.

Kenya said in a statement: "Due to recent and ongoing circumstances, I can no longer continue in the marriage."

She continued on by explaining she would be focusing on her daughter, she said: "My sole concern and focus is and will always be my daughter, Brooklyn, my miracle baby.

"She was made in love and true commitment. I ask for our privacy to be respected moving forward."

Their split comes two years after they secretly exchanged vows and a year after they had their daughter, Brooklyn.

Although it's been reported that Marc wants nothing to do with Kenya, the two set their differences aside to celebrate their daughter's first birthday two months ago.

According to Radar Online, the estranged couple should be having an explosive reunion sometime this season.
