Publish date: 0001-01-01

ADMIT it - there is NOTHING more sacred to children of the 90s than the Toy Story films. Nothing.

Well for reasons we can't understand, Fashion Nova has brought out some VERY suggestive Halloween costumes inspired by our favourite animation series.

  • New Sheriff in Town 4 Piece Costume Set, £48 from Fashion Nova - buy now

And we're not even exaggerating when we say they might just ruin our favourite movie of all time.

All priced at an eye-watering £48 each, the American e-tailer has brought out costumes inspired by Woody, Buzz and the Pizza Planet aliens.

That said, Fashion Nova is careful NOT to go ahead and name the characters - instead calling the costumes"New Sheriff in Town", "Infinite & Beyond" (not even "Infinity) and "Ooooh The Claw" inspired by the Alien's time at the arcade.

Where the cowboy costume has suggestive suspenders and revealing zip-up shirt, the Buzz Lightyear-inspired two-piece is effectively just a bondage bikini with green and white gloves and a purple bra.

  • Infinte And Beyond 6 Piece Costume Set, £48 from Fashion Nova - buy now
  • Ooooh The Claw 4 Piece Costume Set, £48 from Fashion Nova - buy now

Meanwhile, the figure-hugging Alien outfit comes with a navy metallic leotard, neon green gloves and matching fishnet tights.

Unsurprisingly, the outfits have divided opinion on Instagram - with many users claiming they've ruined their childhood.

One user joked: "Can you guys make costumes that aren't so revealing? It's cold on Halloween!"

Another added: "Childhood ruined..."

Meanwhile, a third pondered: "I wonder if people really buy these and go trick or treating."
