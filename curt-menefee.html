Career

Before joining Fox Sports full-time, Curt was a sports reporter for MSG Network’s SportDesk show. Earlier than that, he was the sports anchor for WNYW, New York City’s Fox flagship station.

Menefee made his appearance on-air on WTLV in Jacksonville, Florida, and hosted a radio show on the famous Dallas-based sports radio station KTCK (“1310 The Ticket”).

Moreover, the host also worked as a sports anchor and reporter at WISC-TV (CBS) in Madison, Wisconsin, and for Dallas-Fort Worth’s then-independent station and now CBS affiliate KTVT.

Fox Sports

The 58-year-old started his career at FOX Sports as a sideline reporter, then moved to play-by-play for Fox’s NFL Europe and Fox NFL coverage in 1977.
