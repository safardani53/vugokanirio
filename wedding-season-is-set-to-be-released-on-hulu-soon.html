Wedding Season Is Set To Be Released On Hulu Soon

Publish date: 2024-02-28

Wedding Season, Hulu’s planned action comedy series, will premiere on the site on September 8, 2022, at 12:01 a.m. ET. The plot revolves around a young lady who, while being engaged, has an affair with another guy. Rosa Salazar stars as the series’ protagonist, with Gavin Drea and Jade Harrison, among many others, playing important supporting parts. Continue reading to learn more about Wedding Season.

Wedding Season promises to be unique.

Wedding Season is set to launch on Hulu on September 8, 2022. On the same day, all eight episodes of the series will be accessible for streaming. The plot revolves around a lady who has an affair while being engaged to another guy. When her fiancé is slain on her wedding day, things take a tragic turn. On August 11, 2022, Hulu released the series’ official trailer, which shows the tumultuous life of the protagonist character, Katie. Katie’s fiancé and his whole family are slain, and the police assume Katie’s boyfriend, Stefan, is the perpetrator.

Overall, the teaser combines aspects of a crime thriller, action, and humor, which might make for an entertaining watching experience. Viewers may expect a truly entertaining series full of eccentric characters. According to Hulu Press, the series’ official summary is as follows:

“The genre-bending series follows Katie and Stefan, who meet at a wedding and fall in love, despite Katie already having a fiancé. Katie’s new husband and his whole family are killed two months later during her wedding. Stefan is suspected by the police. Stefan believes Katie did it. And no one is certain what the truth is.”

More information about the Wedding Season cast

In Wedding Season, Rosa Salazar plays the primary role of Katie. Salazar seems to be humorous and lovely in the teaser for the show, and it will be fascinating to watch how her character develops. Salazar is most recognized for her roles on Parenthood, American Horror Story: Murder House, and Undone, in addition to Wedding Season. Alita: Battle Angel, The Divergent Series: Insurgent, The Kindergarten Teacher, and many more are among her cinematic credits.

Gavin Drea, who plays Stefan opposite Salazar, looks similarly great in the trailer. Drea commands the clip with his comedic charm and commanding screen presence. This brilliant actor will provide an engaging performance for viewers. Drea is well-known for his contributions to films such as My Mother and Other Strangers, Valerian and the City of a Thousand Planets, and The Alienist.

In addition to Salazar and Drea, the film stars:

  • Jade Harrison as DCI Metts
  • Bhav Joshi as Anil
  • Omar Baroud as Jackson
  • Jamie Michie as DI Donahue
  • Callie Cooke as Leila

liver Lyttelton, well known for creating the highly acclaimed short film The Listener, devised the program. Wedding Season will be available on Hulu on Thursday, September 8, 2022.
