Best Dressed of the Week

Publish date: 2024-01-30

Rob Kim/Getty

The red carpet got a reality (show) check this week, as more than 12,000 StyleWatch readers gave The Hills star, Audrina Patridge, top honors on our best dressed list.

Instead of sporting her signature skin-tight minis and sky-high platforms, Patridge was still sexy, but more sophisticated in a black halter with two high slits at the End of Watch premiere in Los Angeles. She teamed the understated dress with equally chic accents, including strappy sandals, a long pendant and a red mani.

The reality star even upstaged retro-fashionista Lana Del Rey, who came in a close second thanks to her ladylike white Mulberry sheath worn to the fashion house’s runway show at London Fashion Week.

Though we’ll have to wait and see how long Patridge’s makeunder lasts, we hope it sticks — because the new look is definitely working.

Find out who else rounded out the top five and vote on your favorite looks of the week over on our Red Carpet Spotlight. Tell us: Who do you think was best dressed this week?

–Jennifer Cress
