Resurrected on Instagram! At Last!

Publish date: 2024-07-24

Audrey Roloff has returned to social media.

The Little People, Big World issued a lengthy goodbye statement on Instagram last month for one very exciting reason:

She and husband Jeremy needed time away to finish their first-ever book!

This book will apparently mix stories from their own lives with helpful suggestions and pieces of advice they feel qualified to dole out as two people very much in love.

"Thank you all in advance for respecting our time away, and we would be forever grateful if you would join us in prayer over our book as we finish writing," Audrey wrote back when she confirmed her hiatus.

The popular TLC star added at the time:

"Please pray that the Holy Spirit would inspire or writing, and that this book would be a tool to inspire, encourage, challenge, and change peoples view of love, approach to dating, and desire for marriage.

"LOVE YOU GUYS! Keep on ALWAYS believing in the MORE that is with you. Bye for now."

Now, however, after three painful weeks for her followers, Audrey is saying hello once again.

"After taking 3 weeks completely off social media (which was so necessary and refreshing) WE’RE BACK!" wrote Audrey on Tuesday afternoon, adding:

"Happy Resurrection Day a few days late!

"While we were gone… Our baby got a little bigger, and hair got a little longer;) We also turned in our book manuscript a couple days ago."

Along with this caption, Audrey included a handful of new photos, some of which we’ve shared above and below.

She also added the following words:

Thank you to everyone who has been praying for us as we’ve been in the trenches of writing. We still have a long process of editing to look forward to in the coming months, but we are celebrating this first big milestone in the process!

Jeremy and I are so excited to share this labor of love with all of you.

Our hope is that it would encourage our generation to prepare for, pursue, and protect their love story.

It’s unclear just when Audrey and Jeremy’s book will be available to purchase, but this process takes time.

If they only just turned in a manuscript, we’re guessing the book itself may not even be out until 2019. But we can’t say for certain.

Thankfully, along with Audrey’s social media return, she and her loved ones have also returned to TLC.

A new episodes of their beloved reality show aired on Tuesday night, with plenty more to come in the weeks ahead.
