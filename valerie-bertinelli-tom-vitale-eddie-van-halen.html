Who is Valerie Bertinelli's ex-husband, Tom Vitale?

Publish date: 2024-02-12

AFTER filing for divorce from rocker Eddie Van Halen, Valerie Bertinelli tied the knot with Tom Vitale in 2011.

Despite the picture-perfect romance, on September 28, 2022, the pair officially settled their divorce after a decade of marriage.

Who is Valerie Bertinelli's ex-husband Tom Vitale?

Tom Vitale is an American-born financial planner, TV producer, internet personality, and entrepreneur. 

In 2011, Bertinelli and Vitale famously launched the e-commerce company Veebo alongside Ed Loew, however, the company reportedly closed in 2018.

He has also worked as a producer and co-producer in many films.

Vitale proposed to the actress in March 2010 in Italy, and the lovebirds then got married on January 1, 2011, in their Malibu home.

The wedding ceremony was graced by more than a hundred guests including Bertinelli’s ex-husband, Eddie Van Halen.

When did Bertinelli and Vitale get divorced?

It was announced on May 16, 2022, that Bertinelli had officially filed for divorce from Vitale.

According to Today, she filed the paperwork on May 12 at the Los Angeles Superior Court of California and cited “irreconcilable differences” as the reason for their split.

The outlet later went on to note that the soon-to-be exes had actually been separated since December 2019, and there was a prenuptial agreement “in full force and effect and fully binding on the parties.” 

As news started to spread, fans became worried about Bertinelli but the Hot In Cleveland alum took to social media to put them at ease.

“I’m just having a very bad day,” she said in an Instagram video.

“But you know what? Sometimes we have bad days and then we get over them. And then there’s a good day. So, I’m looking forward to the good day.”

As of September 28, the pair officially settled their divorce with private judge Hank Goldgerg, as per Today.

Do Tom and Valerie have children together?

Despite being together for over a decade, Bertinelli and Vitale never had any children together.

Bertinelli's only child is from her previous marriage to Van Halen.

Wolfgang Van Halen, 31, was born in Santa Monica, California, in 1991.

In 2020, Bertinelli and her son were the first to confirm the news of Van Halen's tragic passing in a tribute on Instagram.
