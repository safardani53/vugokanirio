What did Derrick Jaxn say about his marriage?

On December 10, 2022, Derrick announce on Instagram that he and his wife had divorced.

Making the decision to file was one of the hardest decisions of my life, but I've found peace knowing that our mission of raising healthy children, starting with healthy and whole parents is still being accomplished."

I was blessed to have such an amazing person in my life and will forever be thankful for all she's meant to our family."
