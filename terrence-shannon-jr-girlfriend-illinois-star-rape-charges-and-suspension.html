Terrence Shannon Jr Rape Charges: Suspended Announced 

The 23-year-old Terrence Shannon Jr has been charged with rape for an alleged incident that happened in September. The incident took place in Kansas, where he had gone to watch the football team in action. 

The University of Illinois suspended Shannon from all team activities on December 27, a day after the Douglas (Kansas) County District Attorney issued a warrant for his arrest.

Terrence wasn’t part of the school’s traveling party when the incident happened on September 8. 

Illinois said on Thursday that it had been aware since late September about the charges Shannon was facing. His attorney, Mark P. Sutter, has said his client is innocent of all charges. 

Sutter said that since September, Shannon has cooperated with law enforcement and declared his innocence from the beginning. The attorney added that his client voluntarily surrendered less than 24 hours after the formal charges were filed against him in Lawrence, Kansas.
