GUY and Lori Fieri have welcomed multiple kids over the years, and now fans want to know how many he has.

His oldest son, Hunter, made headlines for his Thanksgiving engagement in November 2023.

How many kids does Guy Fieri have?

When Guy is not traveling the country for Diners, Drive-Ins and Dives, he can be found at home, spending time with his family.

One year after marrying his wife, he welcomed his son Hunter.

In 2005, he welcomed his second son, Ryder, making him a father of two.

Guy and Lori are also the guardians of Guy's nephew Jules from his late sister Morgan.

Over the years, the Fieri kids have kept a low profile, but are often seen on his television shows.

In 2018, Hunter, the self-proclaimed "Prince of FlavorTown," opened up about his relationship with his father, revealing that he wants to follow in his footsteps.

“I didn’t really like anything else,” he told Delish.

“I liked to be outside, and I liked construction and what not, but cooking is what it is. It’s what I do. It’s what we all do.”

“I want to follow in the same path because I feel like I can kill it with that. I want it all,” he continued.

“I want to open my own restaurants — to have things that I created and things my dad created and run this empire.”

In November 2023, Hunter also made headlines for his personal life.

On Thanksgiving, the "Prince of Flavor Town" proposed to his girlfriend, pickleball pro Tara Bernstein, and she said, "yes."

Hunter shared the news on his Instagram, posting photos of the proposal.

He captioned the post: "Coming soon…Mr. & Mrs.Fieri" next to a red heart emoji and the hashtag "#blessed."

The duo started dating in February 2023.

Guy was quick to congratulate the happy couple.

He posted a photo on his own Instagram, captioning the picture: "You two are made for each other. Welcome to the family, Tara."

Hunter's younger brother, Ryder, commented on his brother's post: "Proud lil bro here" alongside a red heart emoji.

Ryder has also been seen multiple times on Guy’s Grocery Games and Guy Fieri’s Family Union

Both of Guy's kids also make frequent appearances on his social media pages.

Who is Guy Fieri's wife?

For over 20 years, Guy has been married to Lori, who he met in 1992 at a Long Beach, California, restaurant.

At the time, he was working as a manager.

In an interview with PEOPLE, she revealed that when they met, he had no goatee, wore a suit every day, and had dark hair.

"Now I look at him and I’m like, ‘Where’s that man I married with the whole clean look?'" Lori joked.

"He's a fantastic person. At the end of the day, I look at him and I still genuinely like him."

She added: "Starting from the time we wake up in the morning, he pretty much has me laughing."

"Our perfect nights are really barbecuing in the backyard and the kids swimming in the pool, we just hunker down at home."

Lori was born on May 31, 1971, in New Providence, Rhode Island.

At this time, little information is available on her occupation and personal life as she tends to stay out of the spotlight.
