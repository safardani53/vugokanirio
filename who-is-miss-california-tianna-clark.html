Who is Miss California 2023 Tianna Clark?

Publish date: 2024-08-11

TIANNA Clark scooped the prestigious Miss California 2023 award.

But who is this year's glamorous winner and how much do we know about her?

Who is Tianna Clark?

Tianna Clark is an American model and actress from Perris who competes in beauty pageants.

She is also a California State University, Fullerton, graduate, and earned her associate’s degree in psychology from Norco College in Riverside County. 

She is said to have started doing runway back in 2006.

Tianna previously represented Eastvale at Miss California USA 2021 and finished in the Top 17.

While in March 2023 she walked the runway at the Los Angeles Fashion Week Festival.

The beauty queen is also a Sergeant in the Army National Guard.

When did Tianna Clark win Miss California 2023?

Tianna was proudly crowned Miss California USA 2023 on June 18, 2023.

The model, 28, was honoured at the Hyatt Regency in Garden Grove, California.

She became the 80th woman to be crowned Miss California.

A strong advocate for diversity and inclusion, she plans to utilize her platform to uplift STEM education and increase mental health awareness.

She hopes to empower women in male dominated environments.

Tianna will now represent California at the Miss USA pageant 2023, which is being held at Grand Sierra Resort in Reno, Nevada.

Who were the Miss California 2023 runner-ups?

Tianna beat 86 contestants from across "The Golden State" to claim the coveted title.

And her fellow contestants are likely to return to battle it out for the crown next year.
