Victoria Beckhams pricey fashion range on sale at TK Maxx

Publish date: 2024-08-27

VICTORIA Beckham’s pricey fashion range is on sale for rock-bottom prices at budget shop TK Maxx.

Posh Spice’s leather boots usually cost more than £1,000 in her London shop — but previous seasons’ stock is now available for less than £200.

Eight designs are up for grabs at TK Maxx — including shoes and boots that are still on sale on other upmarket fashion websites.

Her light grey leather thigh boots originally sold on websites for £1,390.

But TK Maxx has them for £249.99.

White leather heeled ankle boots, originally £850, will cost you £199.99 from the high street retailer.

Other VB shoes on sale include yellow suede stiletto heels for £109.99 and multi-colour panelled platform sandals for £249.99.

Last month, it was revealed that Victoria, 46, earned more money from her pop career than fashion last year.

Her music firm Moody Productions netted £3.3million — while her fashion firm made pre-tax losses of £16.6million.

It also cut 20 staff, a fifth of its workforce, last year in response to Covid-19.
