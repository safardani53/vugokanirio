Church-going 'doting dad', 24, stabbed to death in street in third fatal stabbing in UK just 48 hour

Publish date: 2024-06-10

THIS is the first picture of a 24-year-old father stabbed to death in a quiet residential street near Birmingham.

The boxer and 'doting father' named by friends as Leneto Kellengbeck was the third person to be fatal stabbed in the UK in just 48 hours as the knife crime epidemic continues.

The father-of-one was pronounced dead at the scene on Friday shortly before midnight after cops rushed to Smith's Wood, and a murder probe has now been launched.

Tributes to the boxer have flooded his social media pages as grieving friends shared memories of him as a doting father.

One tribute read: "Rest in peace Leneto, the world is a cruel place and I can't believe this news.

"Thank you for being such a great Dad. You have a beautiful family. I am so devastated my best friend has lost the love of her life, the father to her child over knife crime it's disgusting.

"Thoughts are with all your family and friends Len, fly high."

Another friend who left flowers at the scene told how Leneto was due to have a boxing match on Friday.

They said: "He was a lovely kid.

"He doted on his daughter. He went to church every Sunday. He was a church goer and lived round here."

They added: "He raised a lot of money for Birmingham Children's Hospital - he wanted to help out. He was a good person."

A cordon remains in place to allow for investigations to be carried out in the area. No arrests have been made so far.

Detective Inspector Michelle Allen, from West Midlands Police, said: "A man has sadly lost his life and we are working hard to establish the circumstances around what happened.

"The investigation is in its early stages, and we're keen to hear from anyone who can help us piece together the circumstances leading up to the attack.

"If you were in Arran Way at the time, please contact us as you may have vital information about what happened."

The shocking incident came just hours after dad-of-three Gavin Garrway was knifed in Clapham south London.

The dad-of-three was stabbed to death in front of terrified shoppers in broad daylight on Friday.
