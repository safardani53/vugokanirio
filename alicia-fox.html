Alicia Fox Biography

Publish date: 2024-08-14

Alicia was once in love affairs with Wade Barrett. Wade Barrett is also a professional wrestler from Britain and he is also an actor. They dated each other for several months but they are now separated.

Before Alicia established herself as a wrestler, she also worked as a model. In 2006, she signed a contract with World Wrestling Entertainment and began playing in Ohio Valley Wrestling. On the 20th of October 2006, she became the champion of the gauntlet match at an OVW house show but she lost this title the following day. On 2nd of September 2007, she made her WWE debut and she defeated The Bella Twins. In July 2008, she played her first match in SmackDown. She has also won WWE Divas Championship once. She made her acting debut in 2013. She guest appeared in the TV series, Total Divas. That year she also appeared in the competition series, Cupcake Wars. She is also known for her appearance in the television series, Dominion. Her salary is $180,000 and her net worth is said to be around $2 Million.
