Publish date: 2024-09-21

Mandy Moore grew up the daughter of a pilot, so she has a few travel icks and she isn't afraid to share them.

"Nothing horrifies me more than people fully taking off shoes and socks on the plane. That is just the biggest no-no in my book," Moore said. She admitted that she sometimes takes her shoes off while she's in her seat during a long or international flight.
