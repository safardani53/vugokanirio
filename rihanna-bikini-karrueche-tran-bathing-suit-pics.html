Rihanna’s Bikini In Brazil:

Rihanna, 25, posed in a gorgeous red swim suit with best friend Melissa Forde in Brazil — and she quickly took to Instagram to show it off, posting the sexy picture with the hashtag #WCW, (which stands for ‘Women Candy Wednesday’). RiRi’s body was looking  absolutely flawless, as her toned tummy stole the show in this shot. She looked like she was having so much fun, taking playful shots at the beach. The red suit popped on her bronze skin and her dark hair hung loose in curls — we are totally digging her dark locks, and it was a really cool contrast next to the bright, vibrant suit. The gorgeous two-piece drew so much attention to her toned stomach and beautiful curves, as the cutouts on the bottoms of the suit were super hot and totally in this season. The backdrop of Brazil totally helped make these photos look so awesome.

Karrueche was ready to compete with the Caribbean queen as the 25-year-old also suited up and stripped down for a bikini selfie — and she did so after RiRi posted her sexy snapshot.
