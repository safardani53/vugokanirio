Bachelorette Gabby Windey and Jersey Shore star Vinny Guadagnino were spotted holding hands during a flirty night out for a Dancing With the Stars tour stop, The U.S. Sun has exclusively learned.

She told media outlets at the time that she "could use a pick-me-up" when asked if she was "open to dating" Vinny.
