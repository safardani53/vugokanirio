Who is Love Luxury owner Emily Abraham?

Publish date: 2024-07-28

EMILY Abraham has captured the interest of millions of TikTok users with her expensive taste in fashion.

The 45-year-old is the co-founder of the pre-loved luxury store Love Luxury.

Who is Emily Abraham?

Emily Abraham, 45, is known on social media as the Billionaire's Wife.

She is the co-founder of Love Luxury, a pre-loved luxury store in Knightsbridge, London.

At the age of six, she was raised by her foster mother after her mother died.

She stated on the Emily Abraham Podcast that she was in bed with her biological mother when she passed away.

Speaking to Elaine, her foster mother on the podcast, she said: “You did teach me a lot of very valuable life lessons.

“And also how to be loved and to show love.”

Who is Emily Abraham's husband?

Emily is married to 36-year-old entrepreneur Adam Abraham.

He co-founded Love Luxury with Emily.

He has a YouTube channel with over 300 subscribers, where he teaches his viewers everything he knows about business.

You can find his channel under the username @Mr Adam Abraham

Does Emily Abraham have any children?

Emily is a mum of three.

She keeps the identity of two of her children private, but her youngest is known as the billionaire's daughter.

Emily told Fabulous: “My daughter’s favourite thing to wear is her Yeezy 350 trainers.

“She has a special collection of designer handbags and fine jewellery, the majority of which are family heirlooms. 

“She loves handbags and has such a great knowledge of them already that she’s picked up when she’s spent time in the store.

“She loves to be involved in the business and is so interested in designer fashion now.

“Like most 10-year-olds she loves food and sweets but she has also developed quite a refined palate.

“She loves seafood and her favourite food is gold leaf wasabi prawns.

“We love to visit Harrods together to pick out special outfits, she does love Zara too.”

What is Emily Abraham's net worth?

While Emily Abraham's net worth is unknown, it is clear that she can afford the finer things.

She has an enormous collection of Hermès Birkin bags and spends her days shopping in Harrods, going on spa days and getting her nails done.

She always looks glamorous in chic outfits and wears Rolex watches and Van Cleef & Arpels jewellery.

Emily and her daughter regularly take shopping trips to Harrods, where they buy designer products. 

What is Love Luxury?

Love Luxury is a pre-loved luxury store in London.

Founders Adam and Emily have been bringing authentic luxury goods to buyers for over 15 years.

They buy and sell everything from Hermes, Chanel, Louis Vuitton, Rolex, Cartier, Patek Philippe, Richard Mille, Van Cleef & Arpel and more.
