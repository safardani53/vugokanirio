Sydney Sweeney 'fuming' over co-star Glen Powell's relationship drama with Gigi Paris and denies bei

Publish date: 2024-05-28

ACTRESS Sydney Sweeney is 'fuming' she's been dragged into co-star Glen Powell's relationship drama with his girlfriend Gigi Paris and has insisted to friends she is 'not a homewrecker'.

The Euphoria breakout star, who is engaged to fiance Jonathan Davino, has been embroiled in rumors that she and her co-star Glen are more than colleagues after fans commented on their chemistry.

This month the pair wrapped filming for their upcoming movie, Anyone But You, in Australia, and photographs of them together show Sydney hugging Glen.

The 34-year-old actor had been dating model Gigi Paris, 30, since 2020, but the couple have now reportedly split. 

Gigi unfollowed the actor on Instagram and posted a video of herself with a cryptic message this week which read: "Know your worth & onto the next".

Sydney, 25, has been engaged to her longtime boyfriend, Chicago-based restauranteur Jonathan Davino, since February 2022.

Sydney Sweeney

The couple started dating in 2018.

Speculation has been rife online that her chemistry with Glen may have contributed to his breakup, but she has been telling friends they have not crossed the line.

DATING RUMORS

An LA-based friend of the star exclusively told The U.S. Sun: "Sydney is getting dragged into Glen Powell's drama by no choice of her own.

"She says she has kept things strictly professional with Glen during their short time knowing each other and generally, her M.O. is to not mix her dating life with her work.

"This is a very disciplined, serious young woman who is trying to settle down and get a family going with Jonathan Davino in the next few years.

"Despite the characters she plays, she isn't a 'party girl' and she sure as hell isn't a home-wrecker.

"She doesn't want or need this attention and, to be frank, this is Glen's problem for letting his relationship with Gigi get out of control and for being so public about his personal life on social media."

TMZ previously claimed Sydney is still engaged, with photographs showing her fiance walking her dog Tank this week near their home.

The source went on to add that the movie Sydney and Glen have been working on was only shooting in Australia for around 40 days, and this is a "temporary situation" which has spun out into a "mini-scandal" and is her "worst nightmare."

"If she and Glen were closer, she'd tell him to handle his problems privately instead of letting them bleed into social media," the pal added to The U.S. Sun.

CO-STAR SPLIT

"But the fact is, she and Glen don't have much of a personal relationship at all beyond the making of this movie.

"Glen's real-life girlfriend problems are now infecting how people will perceive this project before they've even had the chance to market it.

"It's distracting and annoying as hell and Sydney loathes that she's been publicly dragged into this."

The U.S. Sun has reached out to Sweeney's rep for comment.

Since Gigi unfollowed her boyfriend on Instagram, a source told PEOPLE the pair split weeks ago, citing Glen's busy schedule as the main issue.

The insider said they had been on the rocks ever since the release of Top Gun: Maverick, which came out in May last year.

The source added: "Gigi was never happy with the long-distance filming and when she came to Australia they both decided to break up for good."
