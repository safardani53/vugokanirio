Baskin-Robbins Tiger Milk Bubble Tea: When does it get added to the menu?

Publish date: 2024-02-01

BASKIN-ROBBINS has added a new treat to their menu for fans to try.

The new drink is called Tiger Milk Bubble Tea and is featured on the ice cream company's website.

If you click on a link in this story we may earn affiliate revenue.

Baskin-Robbins appears to be changing more than just its look, they have added a new drink to the menu.

The Tiger Milk Bubble Tea appeared on the official website on May 9, 2022.

According to a press release, the drink also launched that same day.

The new drink is inspired by Taiwanese milk tea which typically contains tea, milk, and boba.

Read more on The US Sun

Baskin-Robbins Tiger Milk Tea also contains what appears to be the tapioca balls known as boba.

The company website reveals that the drink contains "Black tea concentrate... creamy milk and brown sugar popping bubbles."

This bubble tea will of course feature ice cream as it is being made by the iconic ice cream franchise.

Topped with a scoop of vanilla ice cream and brown sugar syrup to create the "tiger stripes."

Where can I buy the Tiger Milk Bubble Tea?

Bubble tea and ice cream fans alike can pick up one of the new drinks at their local Baskin-Robbins across the United States.

Customers can use the online store locator to find if their nearest store is offering bubble tea.

Baskin-Robbins posted about the drink on its Instagram on May 9, revealing the new drink to its followers.

"Work 🚫 School 🚫. Trying the new Tiger Milk Bubble Tea should be your new #1 priority. 🐯🍵"

In light of the news many fans replied with comments about the bubble tea.

One user wrote: "Has anyone tried it OHMYGOSH???"

While another commented: "My jaw just dropped"

In response to one user asking if it was a joke, the official Baskin-Robbins account wrote: "this 👏 is 👏 not 👏 a 👏 drill"
