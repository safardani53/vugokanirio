Kathy Griffin files for divorce from Randy Bick just days before 4th wedding anniversary after on-of

Publish date: 2024-09-06

KATHY Griffin and husband Randy Bick have called it quits and are headed for divorce just days before their fourth anniversary.

The comedian reportedly filed divorce papers on Thursday citing irreconcilable differences.

Documents obtained by TMZ showed that the former couple had a prenup in place.

Kathy, 63, does not share any children with the marketing executive, who is 18 years younger than her.

After having an on-and-off relationship since 2011, the two finally tied the knot on New Year's Day 2020.

The ceremony took place at her California home and was officiated by comedian Lily Tomlin.

This is the Emmy Award winner's second divorce, previously calling it quits with Matt Moline in 2006 after being married for five years.
