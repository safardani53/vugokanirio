How tall is Pardison Fontaine and how old is he?

Publish date: 2024-10-02

JORDEN Kyle Lanier Thorpe, better known by his stage name Pardison Fontaine, is an American rapper and songwriter.

He is best known for his 2018 single, Backin' It Up, featuring Cardi B, which peaked at number 40 on the US Billboard Hot 100.

What makes Pardison Fontaine stand out of the crowd?

In an interview with GQ back in 2018, Pardison Fontaine’s height was confirmed to be 6 feet 5 inches.

It wasn't too long after he released his debut mixtape, Not Supposed to Be Here, that he began co-writing songs with well-named artists like Cardi B, Kanye West, and G-Eazy.

He has also released albums of his own. UNDER8ED was his first album release. It had 15 tracks and grew inspiration from his in New York.

In 2021, he won the Grammy Award for Best Rap Song and a Grammy Award for Best Rap Performance for his contribution to Savage Remix.

How old is Pardison Fontaine?

Pardison is currently 32 years old.

He was born on December 28, 1989, in Newburg, New York. He grew up in a Christian household, and he regularly attended church and participated in the church choir group.

When he was in the 7th grade, Pardison rapped for the first time and began his music career.

What is Pardison's family life?

Pardison is a private individual and does not share much about his family and relationships with the public.

He does have one daughter named Jordy Junior.

She was born in November of 2016. As of right now, he is happily dating American rapper, Megan Thee Stallion.

They confirmed their relationship in February 2021.

Megan Thee Stallion has said in the past that Pardison "makes me very happy."

"He definitely takes care of me emotionally," she said.

"He makes my brain feel good. He makes my heart feel good," she continued.
