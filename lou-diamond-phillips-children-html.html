Lou Diamond Phillips Children with Ex Wife Kelly Preston Are All Grown Up

Publish date: 2024-03-26

La Bamba actor Lou Diamond Phillips' marriage to model and television extra Kelly Preston was his second take in love.

He was first married to Julie Cypher, an assistant director of one of his movies. They tied the knot in 1987 and parted in 1990. Three years after, Phillips met Preston, and the duo fell in love.

Without wasting time, the couple tied the knot on April 24, 1994, and, eventually, welcomed three daughters into their family — twin Gracie and Isabella, and Lili Jordan. 

Sadly, their marriage didn't stand the test of time. After almost 11 years of marriage, the husband and wife duo divorced in July 2007, sharing joint custody of their children.

Now, fast forward to fourteen years, all their daughters have grown up to be beautiful women. Here's a rundown on their professional endeavors and also personal life. 

Gracie Phillips

Born on 5 October 1997, Gracie is making her parents proud as an up-and-rising model. The 23-year-old is signed to high-profile modeling agencies such as One Management and Next Model Management Miami. 

She has already modeled professionally for five years and worked with major brands such as Too Faced Cosmetics, South Beach swimsuits, and Pat McGrath.

In December 2020, Gracie achieved a milestone, appearing on the cover of the prestigious Sports Illustrated.

"@si_swimsuit is a DREAM COME TRUE and a huge accomplishment for me," she wrote in a celebratory post on her Instagram on December 4, 2020. 

While she is chasing her dream on the professional front as a model and a body positivity advocate, the stunning model is also enjoying her life on the personal front.

According to her Instagram, she has been dating baseball player Blake Rutherford, an outfielder for the Chicago White Sox, since January 2019. 

Isabella Phillips

Isabella, who goes by Bella on her socials, lives in Los Angeles. She is not pursuing modeling as her twin sister, Gracie, but she is making her family proud in her own ways. 

She took part in Paulie's Online Variety Show — live music and improv comedy program on YouTube — twice in January 2021. Besides entertainment, she graduated with honors from college in May 2021. 

If her Instagram is anything to go by, Isabella is not dating anyone at the moment.

She has provided no information about her boyfriend or relationship. That being said, she could be keeping her dating details under wraps, but only time will tell. 

Lili Jordan Phillips

Phillips' youngest daughter, Jordan, was born on September 26, 1999. She turned 21 in 2020, and her oldest sister posted a heart-warming message for her through an Instagram post. 

Stating she was proud of Jordan, Gracie praised her as a beautiful person inside and out. The talented model also mentioned she was wise for her age.

And just like Gracie said, Jordan is a life, style, and consignment specialist at the famous online and brick-and-mortar marketplace, The RealReal.

As for her personal life, she is dating a musician named Kevin, professionally known as Sk1ttles.

Besides three children, Phillips also has another daughter named Indigo Sanara with his now-spouse, Yvonne Boismier. Born in 2007, Sanara is 13 years old.
