When does M&amp;M'S Caramel Cold Brew come out?

Publish date: 2024-05-13

COFFEE lovers across the country are preparing for the launch of M&M'S caramel cold brew flavor.

The popular candy company announced the new flavor in October 2022, and now many want to know when it will be available for purchase.

When does M&M'S Caramel Cold Brew come out?

On October 4, Mars announced a new M&M cold brew flavor, which has been described as a "delicious combination of smooth, robust coffee flavor and sweet, chewy caramel, covered in classic M&M'S milk chocolate and wrapped in a colorful, candy shell."

"More and more coffee drinkers are exploring new flavor combinations, with cold brew ranking as one of Gen Z's most purchased drinks," the company said in a statement.

"Mars' M&M'S brand decided to tap into the booming cold brew coffee scene and pair it with a confectionary favorite - caramel."

"The result? A fun twist to an on-trend favorite that delivers an exceptional coffee flavor experience, all within a classic bag of M&M'S - perfect to enjoy on the go while catching up with friends or sharing a taste of the M&M'S Caramel Cold Brew candies to make new ones." 

The new flavor is scheduled to be released nationwide in February 2023.

How much is M&M'S Caramel Cold Brew?

Starting in February, the cold brew flavor will be available for purchase in three sizes: Single Size (1.41 oz.), Share Size (2.83 oz.), and Sharing Size Stand Up Pouches (9.05 oz).

According to the press release, prices will range between $1.19 and $4.99.

Along with the caramel cold brew flavor, M&M'S will also be releasing a limited-edition holiday Espresso flavor.
