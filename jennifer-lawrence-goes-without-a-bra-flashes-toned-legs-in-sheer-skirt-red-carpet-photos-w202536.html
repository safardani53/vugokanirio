Jennifer Lawrence Goes Without a Bra, Flashes Toned Legs in Sheer Skirt: Red Carpet Photos

Publish date: 2024-05-07

No bra, no problem!

Jennifer Lawrence went without a bra while attending CinemaCon 2016 in Las Vegas on Tuesday, April 12.

The Hunger Games alum, 25, paired a skintight black bodysuit, which she wore sans underwire, with a flowing, sheer Altuzarra skirt that showed off her toned legs. Lawrence finished the black and silver mixed-pattern maxi-length design with strappy black Sophia Webster sandals and jewelry by Beth Miller Collection, Meira T and Ariel Gordon.

The Oscar winner was in Vegas for a panel on her new film, Passengers, in which she stars alongside Chris Pratt. The duo play a pair of futuristic opposites — Lawrence is a writer from New York who wants to tell the tale of a 120-year journey in space firsthand, while the Jurassic World star, 36, is a washed-up handyman whose only wish is to leave Earth.

As the actors prepared to leave the stage, Pratt fumbled awkwardly with the equipment and ended up putting Lawrence’s heels in danger. “Oww, you dropped [the mic] on my foot!” she said, while Pratt responded sheepishly, “That’s the anti-Kanye. I dropped [it] on your foot.”
