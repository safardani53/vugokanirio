The Good Doctor season 3 spoilers: Episode 18 promo shows Shauns heartbreak from Leas rejectio

Publish date: 2024-03-07

AFTER laying it all on the line and telling Lea (Paige Spara) how much he loves her, Shaun (Freddie Highmore) is inconsolable after her rejection in the next The Good Doctor.

The preview for Monday’s new episode of the ABC drama finds everyone trying to help him before realizing he needs some tough love.

In the promo video, Shaun tells his co-worker and friend Claire (Antonia Thomas) how “special” Lea was while he sits on the couch in his apartment and sulks with a blanket wrapped around himself.

She simply responds: “I’m sorry she hurt you.”

When her kind words aren’t enough, Shaun sits with Dr. Glassman (Richard Schiff) for a bit more advice and support.

He explains to his mentee: “What you’re going through, every other young man has gone through.”

Apparently, nothing seems to work to cheer Shaun up because he later yells to Claire at hospital: “I don’t feel better. I’m going home!”

He storms out of the facility and heads to wait at the outside bench by his bus stop, but before he can head home, Dr. Melendez (Nicholas Gonzalez) catches up with him.

Shaun’s boss sternly tells him: “Go back in there, or you won’t be my resident or anyone else’s.”

Whether or not he goes back in is not shown, but the synopsis shares more details about what fans can expect from Monday’s episode (titled “Heartbreak”), revealing: “Dr. Claire Browne and Dr. Shaun Murphy treat a patient with a rare form of dwarfism.

“Meanwhile, Dr. Morgan Reznick, Dr. Audrey Lim and Dr. Alex Park treat a young man who had both arms torn off in a previous farming accident, and Shaun reacts to an emotional situation.”

The show’s cast includes Antonia Thomas (Dr. Claire Browne), Nicholas Gonzalez (Dr. Neil Melendez), Hill Harper (Dr. Marcus Andrews) and Richard Schiff (Dr. Aaron Glassman).

Christina Chang (Dr. Audrey Lim), Fiona Gubelmann (Dr. Morgan Reznick), Will Yun Lee (Dr. Alex Park), Paige Spara (Lea Dilallo) and Jasika Nicole (Dr. Carly Lever) also star on the medical drama.

Season 4, episode 18 of The Good Doctor airs on ABC on Monday at 10 p.m.

This is the last episode before the two-part season 3 finale.
