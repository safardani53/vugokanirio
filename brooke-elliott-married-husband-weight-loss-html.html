How old is Brooke Elliot?

Born on 16 November 1974 in Fridley, Minnesota, Elliot’s age as of writing is 48. She has been singing since the age of three. 

Brooke Elliot’s short bio

Brooke Elliot is one of three offspring of city manager father, Robert, and mother, Kathleen Elliott. Her father was a city manager.

As a child, the Drop Dead Diva had quite a life, moving to Blaine, Minnesota, from her birthplace Fridley, Minnesota. Her family later moved to Missouri, Oklahoma, and Illinois. They finally settled in Riverview, Michigan.

In 1993, Elliot received her high school diploma from Gabriel Richard Catholic High School. While there, she took part in various high school productions of classics: Joseph and the Amazing Technicolor Dreamcoat and Richard III.

Pursuing her passion for acting, she enrolled in Western Michigan University’s Bachelor of Fine Arts program in Musical Theatre performance and graduated in 1998.

What is Brooke Elliot doing now?

The singer/actress’ early professional gigs include her minor roles in Babes With Blades, Robin Hood: Prince Of Thieves, The Miracle Worker, and Heathers.

Her major professional theatrical gigs constitute the US touring version of Beauty And The Beast and the North American tour of Wicked alongside Adam Lambert. In addition, she acted in her first Broadway alongside Boy George in Taboo.

In 2016, Elliot starred alongside Katey Sagal in the CBS comedy pilot Upper Middle Bogen which was inspired by the Australian series, and in 2018, she went on to star in the ABC comedy pilot by Bobby Bowman concerning a dysfunctional family struggling with mental illness.

She has recently been known for two onscreen projects: Sweet Magnolias (2019-2022) and the Lifetime film A Country Christmas In Harmony (2022).
