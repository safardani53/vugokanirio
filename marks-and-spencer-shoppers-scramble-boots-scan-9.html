M&amp;S shoppers scramble to get their hands on stylish 50 boots that scan for just 9.99 at the till

Publish date: 2024-07-28

M&S shoppers are scrambling to get their hands on cut-price boots that are not only super stylish, but perfect for Spring too.

One savvy shopper, Orly Lyndon-Brown, was left open-mouthed when shopping in her local M&S in Norwich recently.

Yesterday, the bargain hunter took to Facebook, sharing a picture of a bargain buy that she spotted on sale in the much-loved high-street giant.

She shared the epic purchase in the private group Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK, which has 2.2million members. 

Posting two pictures of the reduced boots, Orly shared her post with the caption: “Lots of lovely boots and clothes in the M&S sales. £10.”

The beautiful cream boots have a lovely zip detail at the front and were originally being sold for £49.50, but had been reduced down to just £15.
