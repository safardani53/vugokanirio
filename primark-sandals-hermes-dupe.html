PRIMARK shoppers are scrambling to get their hands on the new-in sandals that look strikingly similar to those by designer brand Hermès.

So if you want to look expensive but without breaking the bank, you’ll need to listen up.

We all love Primark for the cheap bags, cool clothes and make-up dupes, but now there’s a pair of sandals that have got people talking.

If the thought of spending a whopping £570 on a pair of shoes makes your skin crawl, fear not, as Primark are now selling a dupe pair that are not only strikingly similar, but are also a whopping £563 cheaper too.

Posting on TikTok under the username @hightstreet_closet, the savvy shopper spotted the new-in Sandals in a Primark in the North East of England, in Darlington. 

The social media user recently shared a clip showing off the stunning shoes, leaving many open-mouthed.
